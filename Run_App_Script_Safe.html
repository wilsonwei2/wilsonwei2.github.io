How to Safely Run Scripts in Google Sheets

⚠️ To avoid accessing or modifying your personal Google Drive files, please switch to the system account (e.g. testuser@bohme.com or PO_TOOL@yourdomain.com)) before using any custom menu or script.

Step 1: Open the Google Sheet Or it is already open via link from web portal

Navigate to the shared Google Sheet you want to work with.

Step 2: Click Your Profile Icon

In the upper-right corner of the page, click your Google account profile image or initial.

Step 3: Select the IT Account

Choose the system account (e.g. testuser@bohme.com or PO_TOOL@yourdomain.com) from the list.

If it’s not listed, click “Add another account” and sign in with the credentials provided.

Step 4: Reload the Google Sheet

After switching, reload the Sheet to ensure all scripts run under the correct account.

Step 5: Use the Menu

You can now safely run scripts using the custom menu provided in the Google Sheet (e.g., “Product Management”, “Load PO”, etc.).
