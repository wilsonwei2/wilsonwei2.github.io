Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 22, 2025

This privacy policy explains how our internal tools — including Google Sheets integrations, Apps Script functions, and AWS-hosted services — use and protect your information.

Information Collected

Our tools may access the following data from your Google account when authorized:

  • Your Google Sheet contents
  • Your basic account information (email address, profile)
  • Drive file name or rename -- those files owned by your IT Admin , but shared with you.
  • New PO file -- those files will be created in your IT admin account folder , but will share with you.

How Information is Used

The data collected is used only for business operations such as:

  • Processing purchase orders (POs)
  • Managing product data and syncing with platforms like Shopify or NetSuite
  • Interacting with internal APIs hosted on AWS

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. All operations are internal and secured via Google Workspace and AWS IAM policies.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures including OAuth 2.0, Google Workspace domain restrictions, and HTTPS for API communication.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, contact the administrator at support@ucghub.net.
